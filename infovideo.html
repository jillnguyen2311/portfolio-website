Info video animation project

Intro

The focal point of this project is the creation of a concise animated informational video designed to enlighten audiences about the consequences of acquiring a pet without adequate care and the vital importance of thoughtful consideration before committing to pet ownership. Through captivating animation and informative content, this project endeavors to raise awareness and encourage responsible decision-making among potential pet owners.
