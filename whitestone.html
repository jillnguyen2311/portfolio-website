Problem

According to Statistics Canada, one in three Canadians doesn't have proper access to dental care â€” an entire 1/3rd of our country. This is the idea that inspired our team to make a change.

WhiteStone is a Machine-learning oral health scanning app that helps individuals who are low-income, colorblind, and unable to get to the dentist regularly. The goal of our app is to help people evaluate their dental health and reduce the number of necessary dental checkups, as well as provide a dental assistant for those who need it.

Team Experience after the project ends

Challenges the Team faced

  • Communication issues among team members
  • Tight schedule that all members had to face due to a heavy course timetable
  • Confusion at the start of the project
  • Deadlines pressure

Solutions to Success

  • Communicate more actively
  • Provide more updates and share even the smallest progress
  • Try to become more organized
  • Set clear objectives and deadlines
