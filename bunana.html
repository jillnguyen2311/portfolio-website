Project Progress

Branding mascot creation

Design a captivating branding mascot that seamlessly merges the adorable charm of a bunny with the playful appeal of a banana. Envision the mascot as a whimsical representation of a rabbit comfortably reclining on a buoyant banana, gracefully floating atop the creamy surface of a delectable milk tea. This imaginative imagery not only evokes a sense of whimsy and delight but also serves as a visual metaphor for the unique blend of flavors and fun awaiting customers in your brand's offerings.
